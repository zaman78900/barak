Imperial Earl Grey

star star star star star
Oct 24, 2023
Verified Buyer Eleanor Vance • London, UK

Exquisite aroma, but delicate brewing required

The bergamot notes are incredibly authentic and not at all synthetic like lesser brands. The gold flecks in the blend make it feel like a true luxury experience. However, be very careful with water temperature. Anything over 90°C will scorch the leaves and bring out a harsh bitterness. At 85°C, it's absolute perfection.
