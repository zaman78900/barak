Our Heritage

Rooted in the Soil of Assam's Hidden Valley

The Barak Valley, a fertile crescent in southern Assam, holds a quiet legacy of tea cultivation. For generations, artisans have perfected the Crush, Tear, Curl method here, extracting deep amber liquors that are notoriously full-bodied.

We preserve this unyielding commitment to quality, bringing the authentic, unblended strength of the valley directly to connoisseurs who demand more from their morning ritual.
