The Art of Brewing

The Perfect Cup
of Chai

Master the ritual of brewing Barak Valley's finest. A guide to patience, proportion, and pure indulgence.

1

Boil Fresh Water

Always start with cold, freshly drawn water. Bring it to a rolling boil (100°C/212°F). Oxygenated water draws out the complex malt notes of our CTC blend.

2

Add Quality Tea

Measure exactly 1 rounded teaspoon (approx. 2.5g) of Barak Valley CTC per cup into your warmed teapot or saucepan.

Recommended

Estate Reserve CTC

arrow_forward
3

Steep & Brew

Pour the boiling water over the leaves. Let it steep for 3-5 minutes depending on desired strength. For an authentic Indian style, simmer gently on the stove.

lightbulb

Pro Tip

Do not rush this step. The robust, malty character of Assam-style tea needs time to fully extract before adding milk.
