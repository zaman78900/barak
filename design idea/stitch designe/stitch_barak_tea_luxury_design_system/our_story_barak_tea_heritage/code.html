The story of BARAK Tea

Nestled in the lush, verdant landscapes of the Barak Valley, our story begins over a century ago. The unique terroir, defined by rich alluvial soil and a humid, subtropical climate, creates the perfect environment for cultivating tea leaves of exceptional quality and character.

"We don't just grow tea; we nurture a legacy that flows through the valley, capturing the essence of the land in every cup."

Our artisanal approach marries traditional wisdom with modern sustainable practices. Every leaf is hand-plucked at the peak of dawn, ensuring that the delicate flavors and robust aromas are preserved, delivering an unparalleled tasting experience that speaks to the soul of Assam.
